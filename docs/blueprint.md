# **App Name**: SocialSync

## Core Features:

- Account Integration: Securely connect to social media accounts (Facebook, WhatsApp, X, Instagram, LinkedIn).
- Unified Inbox: Display a unified inbox showing new messages from all platforms.
- Message Filtering: Filter messages by platform, sender, and date.
- Unified Messaging: Send direct messages and post to groups on connected platforms.
- Media Management: Upload and download media files across connected social networks.
- AI Smart Replies: Smart Reply Suggestion tool: Uses AI to provide quick, context-aware replies based on received messages.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5), evoking trust and connectivity, while maintaining a professional feel suitable for cross-platform social management. It reflects reliability without being overly vibrant.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F2F5), to ensure readability and a clean aesthetic, especially important for a message-heavy interface. Its low saturation minimizes distraction, allowing social content to stand out.
- Accent color: Purple (#7E57C2), serving to highlight interactive elements, such as send buttons and account connection prompts. Its higher brightness ensures visibility without clashing with the primary color.
- Clean, modern sans-serif fonts for readability across different platforms.
- Consistent and recognizable icons for each social media platform.
- Intuitive tab-based navigation for easy switching between accounts and features.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience and provide feedback.
